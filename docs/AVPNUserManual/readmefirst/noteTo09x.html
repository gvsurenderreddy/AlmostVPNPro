Note to 0.9.x Users


As you may already noticed, 1.0 is VERY DIFFERENT from 0.9.x.  I do believe that after a while, you will find it easier to use than 0.9.x. But judging by some people reaction on 1.0, having been "0.9 junkie",  might be a disadvantage. 1.0 can do a lot of very smart things which 0.9.x have not even dreamed about, but at the same time, 1.0 does even most simple things differently compare to 0.9.x.  Please read next chapter (Big Idea) it will help you to adjust to the brave new world of AlmostVPN 1.0.
